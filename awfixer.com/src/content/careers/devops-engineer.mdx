# DevOps Engineer

Join our infrastructure team to build and maintain the systems that power AWFixer Cloud.

## About the Role

As a DevOps Engineer, you'll be responsible for designing, implementing, and maintaining our cloud infrastructure. You'll work on automation, monitoring, and tooling that enables our engineering teams to ship with confidence.

## Responsibilities

- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines for continuous deployment
- Manage cloud infrastructure using Infrastructure as Code
- Implement monitoring, alerting, and observability solutions
- Optimize system performance and cost efficiency
- Respond to incidents and conduct post-mortems

## Requirements

- 3+ years of DevOps or SRE experience
- Strong experience with Kubernetes and container orchestration
- Proficiency with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Experience with IaC tools (Terraform, Pulumi, or similar)
- Scripting skills in Python, Bash, or Go

## Nice to Have

- Experience with service mesh technologies (Istio, Linkerd)
- Security certifications or background
- Experience with GitOps workflows
- Familiarity with observability tools (Prometheus, Grafana, Datadog)

## Benefits

- Competitive salary and equity
- Fully remote work environment
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Unlimited PTO policy
- Learning and development budget
- Conference attendance opportunities
