# Senior Software Engineer

We're looking for an experienced software engineer to join our team and help build the next generation of AWFixer products.

## About the Role

As a Senior Software Engineer at AWFixer, you'll work on challenging problems across our entire stack, from low-level systems programming to modern web applications. You'll collaborate with a talented team of engineers who are passionate about building great software.

## Responsibilities

- Design and implement scalable backend services and APIs
- Contribute to architectural decisions and technical strategy
- Mentor junior engineers and conduct code reviews
- Collaborate with product and design teams to deliver user-focused features
- Participate in on-call rotations and incident response

## Requirements

- 5+ years of professional software development experience
- Strong proficiency in TypeScript, Python, or Rust
- Experience with distributed systems and microservices architecture
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills

## Nice to Have

- Experience with Linux systems programming
- Contributions to open source projects
- Background in security or DevOps
- Experience with container orchestration (Kubernetes)

## Benefits

- Competitive salary and equity
- Fully remote work environment
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Unlimited PTO policy
- Learning and development budget
- Home office stipend
