# Product Designer

We're seeking a talented Product Designer to help shape the future of AWFixer's user experience.

## About the Role

As a Product Designer, you'll be responsible for the end-to-end design of features across our product suite. You'll work closely with engineering, product management, and other stakeholders to deliver intuitive, accessible, and beautiful user experiences.

## Responsibilities

- Lead design projects from concept to implementation
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Establish and maintain design systems and guidelines
- Collaborate with engineers to ensure design quality in production

## Requirements

- 3+ years of product design experience
- Strong portfolio demonstrating user-centered design process
- Proficiency in Figma or similar design tools
- Experience with design systems and component libraries
- Excellent visual design skills and attention to detail

## Nice to Have

- Experience with developer tools or technical products
- Familiarity with front-end development (HTML, CSS, React)
- Motion design and animation skills
- Experience with accessibility standards (WCAG)

## Benefits

- Competitive salary and equity
- Fully remote work environment
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Unlimited PTO policy
- Learning and development budget
- Design tools and software subscriptions
